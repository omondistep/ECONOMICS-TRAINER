I developed this for personal use, but since some of you may find it useful, allow me to share it here. TARAJIO is my NSE stock analysis platform. It gives you real-time insights on Kenyan stocks with:

• NSE market data with daily price updates
• AI-powered stock scoring (0-10 scale) and recommendations
• Advanced technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, Bollinger Bands)
• Fundamental analysis with DCF & DDM valuation models
• Sector analysis and peer comparisons
• Comprehensive downloadable reports.
Fundamental Recommendation answers "Is this a good stock to own?" based on value and financial health. 
Technical Verdict answers "What is the current price momentum saying?" based on charts and indicators.
Signal answers "What should I do?" based on combined insights from Recomendation and Technical verdict.

NB. Not designed for mobile screens.
